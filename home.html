Question A Day Activity

Welcome to QAD, the exciting activity that challenges your math skills every day! QAD, which stands for Questions A Day, was started as a hobby by the amazing Maths Teacher, Subodh Pethe, and has been going strong for nine years now. Several hundreds students have benifited from this activity over the years.

As a QAD participant, you'll receive new math questions every day and get to put your problem-solving abilities to the test. QAD is an engaging activity that offers interesting and challenging math problems for students in standard VI to X. Whether you're in these standards or are simply passionate about math, we welcome you to join our WhatsApp group and take part in the fun!

All you have to do is solve the questions and keep your answers ready. We'll send you the handwritten solutions and new questions the next day. By participating in QAD for a few days, you'll notice an improvement in your math skills and interest in the subject.

If you're ever stuck on a problem, don't worry! You can send us a message on WhatsApp and we'll do our best to help you out.
